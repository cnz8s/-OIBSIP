data science

  • Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines expertise from statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract valuable insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data.
  • It encompasses a range of techniques, including data cleaning, data preprocessing, data visualization, and machine learning algorithms.
  • Data scientists play a pivotal role in understanding complex data patterns, making data-driven decisions, and predicting future trends.
  • The most common way to use AI is by using machine learning algorithms to make decisions based on data. An algorithm is a set of rules or steps that calculate or predict something automatically.
  • For example, an algorithm may take data on the price of gasoline, the number of cars in your fleet, how much time you spend commuting each day, and how healthy you feel when you wake up in the morning and then provide recommendations for how to improve your work-life balance. In this way, data science is applied to AI and vice versa.
